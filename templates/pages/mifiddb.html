    MiFID II/MiFIR

    This page is a curated compliation of MiFID II and MiFIR regulatory updates from the FCA, PRA, ESMA. On 20 October 2011, the European Commission adopted formal proposals for a Directive 2014/65/EU on markets in financial instruments repealing Directive 2004/39/EC (MiFID II) and the Regulation on markets in financial instruments (MiFIR). The directive's objective is to increase competition and consumer protection in financial services.
